Historical Places in Cape Town

City Hall

  • Cape Town city centre which was built in 1905.
  • Nelson Mandela made his first public speech from the balcony of Cape Town City Hall.
  • The city Hall has lived through both world wars.

District Six

  • The floor of the museum is covered with a big map of the district.
  • The District Six museum was found in 1994.
  • The museum offers programmes for current inhabitants to help develop the district.

Castle of Good Hope

  • The Castle is the oldest existing building in South Africa.
  • The Castle was built by soldiers, volunteers, slaves and Khoi undergoing punishment.
  • The Castle was never attacked.
